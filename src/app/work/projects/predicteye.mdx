## Overview

predictye is an AI-powered product price forecasting platform that predicts the future value of physical goods across different time horizons.

From vintage watches to consumer electronics and furniture, the system analyzes:

- Historical market pricing
- Depreciation patterns
- Supply-demand behavior
- Product lifecycle signals

The goal is simple:  
Help users make smarter buy, sell, and hold decisions using predictive intelligence instead of guesswork.

---

## Problem Statement

Product pricing is often reactive and speculative. Buyers and sellers struggle with:

- Uncertain resale value  
- Lack of long-term pricing visibility  
- Manual market research  
- Volatile secondary markets  

predictye addresses this by providing forward-looking price estimates powered by machine learning.

---

## What I Built

### 1. Predictive Pricing Engine

- Designed forecasting models to estimate product prices across multiple timelines (1 year, 5 years, 10 years).
- Integrated market trend analysis and depreciation modeling.
- Engineered features such as:
  - Age of product
  - Brand influence
  - Market demand trends
  - Historical price volatility

The system generates confidence-based price projections instead of static estimates.

---

### 2. AI-Based Depreciation Modeling

- Developed dynamic depreciation curves based on product categories.
- Adjusted decay rates depending on:
  - Product type (luxury, electronics, collectibles)
  - Market behavior
  - Demand shifts
- Enabled long-term value forecasting instead of short-term speculation.

---

### 3. Data Aggregation & Processing Pipeline

- Structured ingestion pipeline for historical product pricing datasets.
- Cleaned and normalized inconsistent marketplace data.
- Created reusable preprocessing modules to standardize prediction inputs.

This ensured reliable model performance across different product categories.

---

### 4. Instant Forecast System

- Built a lightweight inference system to return predictions in seconds.
- Designed minimal input flows to reduce friction.
- Optimized model response time for real-time forecasting.

---

### 5. Trust & Model Evaluation Framework

- Implemented validation strategies to measure:
  - Forecast error
  - Model stability
  - Generalization performance
- Continuously improved accuracy through model tuning and evaluation cycles.

The focus was not just prediction — but dependable prediction.

---

## Architecture

predictye follows a structured pipeline:

1. User inputs product details  
2. System retrieves historical pricing patterns  
3. Feature engineering transforms raw inputs  
4. Forecasting model generates multi-year predictions  
5. UI visualizes price trajectory and confidence metrics  

The architecture supports future expansion into:

- More product categories  
- Real-time marketplace integrations  
- Adaptive learning models  
- Portfolio-style asset tracking  

---

## Key Challenges

### Noisy Marketplace Data  
Resale markets contain inconsistent and incomplete data.  
Built cleaning pipelines and normalization strategies to reduce bias.

### Long-Term Forecast Uncertainty  
Future price estimation involves volatility.  
Balanced predictive power with controlled model assumptions to avoid unrealistic projections.

### User Trust  
Predictions must feel credible.  
Focused on transparency in trends and confidence signals rather than black-box outputs.
